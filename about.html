About

Timik Inc– Timik Inc started as an Infrastructure Company in 2010 with IoT as its core focus. The Telecoms started operations in the year 2012 as a company to provide broad spectrum of world-class telecommunication products and services with major focus on security The company has grown steadily by building capacities harnessing local expertise, international skills and key partnerships aimed at delivery of world class infrastructure solutions. Timik Inc has strong brand value leveraging on competent and professionals hands with vast experience in infrastructure deployment whilst maintaining good relationship with clients.
